# Stitch Brief: GLUCOLIT Prediabetes H5 Redesign

## Product

GLUCOLIT is a mobile H5 demo for people with prediabetes or metabolic risk. It converts health signals, lab reports, meal photos, and ingredient labels into small daily actions.

This is not a generic SaaS dashboard and not a marketing landing page. It should feel like a credible metabolic health companion.

## Reference Site

Original content site: `https://glucolit.vercel.app/zh`

Useful qualities from the original:

- Evidence-based editorial confidence.
- Strong GLUCOLIT brand presence.
- Dark "Today's focus" visual card.
- Topic clusters with clear content hierarchy.
- Professional metabolic-health tone.

Do not copy the site directly because the MVP is an interactive H5 tool, not a long article portal.

## Target User

Chinese-speaking 30-45 year-old prediabetes/metabolic-risk user:

- Has abnormal HbA1c, OGTT, fasting glucose, or post-meal glucose.
- Feels anxious but does not want a scary medical interface.
- Needs direct, small, realistic actions.
- Needs trust and safety boundaries.

## Visual Direction

Keywords:

- evidence briefing
- metabolic monitoring
- calm intervention
- daily action coach
- glucose trend
- credible but warm

Colors:

- Deep ink green as hero/dark card background.
- Metabolic teal for primary actions.
- Mint for completion and safe progress.
- Amber for attention signals.
- Coral only for soft risk hints.
- Off-white app background.

Avoid:

- Purple/blue gradients.
- Decorative blobs.
- Generic SaaS cards.
- Big spreadsheet-like metric grids.
- Fake medical charts that look random.

## Required Mobile Screens

### Home

Top:

- GLUCOLIT logo/wordmark.
- Small evidence-based subtitle.

First visible card:

- A dark "今日关注" / "AI 监控中" hero card.
- Show current state: 需关注.
- Show three concise signals: 餐后峰值 8.6, 睡眠 5.8h, 3 天未打卡.
- Include a believable glucose mini-chart line inside the dark card.
- Include one direct recommendation: "今晚先完成饭后 15-20 分钟步行。"

Below:

- "监控预警" cards with signal / why it matters / action.
- "拍照分析" quick actions: 报告, 餐盘, 配料表.

### AI Tools

Must look like photo scanning/upload:

- Tab switch: 报告解读 / 餐盘分析 / 配料表分析.
- Large scanner-style upload panel.
- Buttons: 拍照上传, 使用样例.
- Results must look reviewed and explainable.

### Actions

Checklist with five daily actions:

- 饮食
- 睡眠
- 运动
- 压力
- 精力

Done state should feel satisfying but not gamified.

## Copy Rules

Use:

- 值得关注
- 建议复查
- 建议咨询医生
- 更适合
- 需控制
- 建议替换
- 今天先完成一个低风险小行动

Never use:

- 诊断
- 治愈
- 逆转
- 保证降低血糖
- 不能吃
- 必须吃
- 建议吃药
- 停药

## Layout Constraints

- Mobile viewport 390x844.
- Desktop centers a phone shell.
- 8px radius.
- Minimum 44px touch targets.
- No nested cards.
- Text must not overflow buttons/cards.
